I transform complex data into clear, actionable insights that drive business growth. With a passion for problem-solving, I specialize in building intuitive dashboards, automating workflows, and uncovering the insights hidden within the numbers.

I have 3.5 years of experience solving business problems through data analysis, automation, and intuitive reporting. My core strengths include building interactive dashboards, automating workflows using Python, and uncovering insights with SQL, Excel, and Power BI.

Along the way, I've worked on impactful analytics projects, streamlined repetitive tasks, and delivered dashboards used by leadership teams for decision-making. I enjoy challenges that require both technical understanding and business acumen.

I am 5x certified in Power BI and Azure.



## Core Competencies

- **Data Analysis & Visualization**: Building interactive dashboards and compelling visual narratives
- **Automation & Scripting**: Python-based workflow automation and process optimization
- **Database & SQL**: Complex queries, data modeling, and analytics engineering
- **Business Intelligence**: Power BI, Excel analytics, and reporting
- **Problem-Solving**: Translating business requirements into technical solutions

## What I Do

- Design and develop interactive dashboards that tell data stories
- Automate repetitive tasks to improve efficiency and reduce manual work
- Conduct deep-dive analyses to uncover actionable business insights
- Collaborate with stakeholders to define analytics requirements
- Build scalable data solutions that support business decisions

## Beyond Work

I'm passionate about continuous learning and sharing knowledge. When I'm not working with data, you'll find me exploring new technologies, writing about data science and analytics, or working on interesting and fun projects.
